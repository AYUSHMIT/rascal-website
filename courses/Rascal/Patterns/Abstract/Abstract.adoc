[[Patterns-Abstract]]
# Abstract Patterns
:concept: Patterns/Abstract

.Synopsis
Abstract patterns.

.Syntax


|====
| Pattern              | Syntax                                                                      

| Literal              | <<Values-Boolean>>, <<Values-Integer>>, <<Values-Real>>, <<Values-Number>>, <<Values-String>>, <<Values-Location>>, or <<Values-DateTime>> 
| Variable declaration | `_Type_ _Var_`                                                              
| Multi-variable       | `*_Var_`, `*_Type_ _Var_`                                                   
| Variable             | `_Var_`                                                                     
| List                 | `[ _Pat~1~_, _Pat~2~_, ..., _Pat~n~_ ]`                                        
| Set                  | `{ _Pat~1~_, _Pat~2~_, ..., _Pat~n~_ }`                                        
| Tuple                | `< _Pat~1~_, _Pat~2~_, ..., _Pat~n~_ >`                                        
| Node                 | `_Name_ ( _Pat~1~_, _Pat~2~_, ..., _Pat~n~_ )`                                 
| Descendant           | `/ _Pat_`                                                                   
| Labelled             | `_Var_ : _Pat_`                                                             
| TypedLabelled        | `_Type_ _Var_ : _Pat_`                                                      
| TypeConstrained      |  `[_Type_] _Pat_`                                                           
|====

.Types

.Function

.Description
An abstract pattern is recursively defined and may contain the following elements:

*** <<Abstract-Descendant,Descendant Pattern>>: Deep match in an abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-Labelled,Labelled Pattern>>: Labelled abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-List,List Pattern>>: List in abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-Literal,Literal Pattern>>: Literal in abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-MultiVariable,MultiVariable Pattern>>: Multi-variable (also known as Splice-variable) in abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-Node,Node pattern>>: Node in abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-Set,Set Pattern>>: Set in abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-Tuple,Tuple Pattern>>: Tuple in abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-TypeConstrained,Type Constrained Pattern>>: Type constrained abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-TypedLabelled,Typed and Labelled Pattern>>: Typed, labelled, abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-Variable,Variable Pattern>>: Variable in abstract pattern.
*** <<Abstract-VariableDeclaration,Variable Declaration Pattern>>: Variable declaration in abstract pattern.
