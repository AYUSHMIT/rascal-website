
[[Number-Addition]]
# Number Addition
:concept: Expressions/Values/Number/Addition

.Synopsis
Addition on numeric values.

.Syntax
`_Exp~1~_ + _Exp~2~_`

.Types


|====
| `_Exp~1~_`  |  `_Exp~2~_` | `_Exp~1~_ + _Exp~2~_` 

| `int`      |  `int`     | `int`                
| `int`      |  `real`    | `real`               
| `real`     |  `real`    | `real`               
|====

.Function

.Description
Yields the numerical sum of the values of _Exp_~1~ and _Exp_~2~.

.Examples
[source,rascal-shell]
----
rascal>12 + 13
int: 25
rascal>12 + 13.5
real: 25.5
----
