
[[Number-Multiplication]]
# Number Multiplication
:concept: Expressions/Values/Number/Multiplication

.Synopsis
Multiply two numeric values.

.Index
*

.Syntax
`_Exp_~1~ * _Exp_~2~`

.Types


|====
| `_Exp~1~_` |  `_Exp~2~_` | `_Exp~1~_ * _Exp~2~_` 

| `int`     |  `int`     | `int`               
| `int`     |  `real`    | `real`              
| `real`    |  `real`    | `real`              
|====

.Function

.Description
Yields the result of multiplying the values of _Exp~1~_ and _Exp~2~_.

.Examples
[source,rascal-shell]
----
rascal>12 * 13
int: 156
rascal>12 * 13.5
real: 162.0
rascal>-12*13
int: -156
----
