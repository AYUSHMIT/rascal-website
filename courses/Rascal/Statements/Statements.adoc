[[Rascal-Statements]]
# Statements
:concept: Statements

.Synopsis

All Rascal statements.


.Syntax

.Types

.Function

.Description

The following statements are available:

** <<Statements-Append,Append>>: Append an element to the list value produced by various loop statements.
** <<Statements-Assert,Assert>>: An executable assertion.
** <<Statements-Assignment,Assignment>>: Assign a value to a variable or more complex data structure.
*** <<Assignment-Annotation,Annotation>>: Assign to annotation.
*** <<Assignment-Constructor,Constructor>>: Assign to constructor.
*** <<Assignment-Field,Field>>: Assign to a field of a tuple, relation or datatype.
*** <<Assignment-IsDefined,IsDefined>>: Assign but replace if value is not defined.
*** <<Assignment-Multiple,Multiple>>: Assign to multiple assignables.
*** <<Assignment-Slice,Slice>>: Assign to a slice of a list or string.
*** <<Assignment-Subscription,Subscription>>: Assign a single element of a structured value.
*** <<Assignment-Variable,Variable>>: Assign to a variable.
** <<Statements-Block,Block>>: Group statements into a block.
** <<Statements-Break,Break>>: End the execution of a while, do or for loop.
** <<Statements-Continue,Continue>>: Continue with the next iteration of while, do or for loop.
** <<Statements-Do,Do>>: .
** <<Statements-Fail,Fail>>: Let the current alternative of a pattern match fail.
** <<Statements-For,For>>: For loop.
** <<Statements-If,If>>: .
** <<Statements-Insert,Insert>>: Insert a value in a tree during a <<Visit>>.
** <<Statements-Return,Return>>: Return a value as result of a [Function].
** <<Statements-Solve,Solve>>: Solve a set of equalities by fixed-point iteration.
** <<Statements-Switch,Switch>>: Switch statement.
** <<Statements-Test,Test>>: Test statement (__deprecated__).
** <<Statements-Throw,Throw>>: Throw an exception.
** <<Statements-TryCatch,Try Catch>>: Try to execute a statement and catch resulting exceptions.
** <<Statements-While,While>>: While loop.
